Who We Are

The DRC's
BTL Experts

Top Marketing Agency (TMA) is a Below The Line (BTL) marketing agency registered and operating in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). Our objective is to offer Below The Line marketing services to Companies within DRC. (Brilliant Sales, Merchandising and Promotions services).

We also help foreign manufacturers, distributors, companies to penetrate, implement and manage their marketing strategies to achieve their business goals in RD Congo market. We partner with brands, both big and small, to help them meet their business goals. We operate in DR Congo market in Goma, Bukavu, Butembo, Bunia, Kisangani, Kalemi, Lubumbashi, Kinshasa, etc).
